DESCRIPTION

Replace org.junit4 by org.junit bundle

The bundle org.junit4 was removed from Kepler [1] hence replace it by
org.junit. The bundle org.junit4 was introduced as a workaround to allow
having both junit 3 and 4 in the same release train prior to Indigo.

[1] http://dev.eclipse.org/mhonarc/lists/cross-project-issues-dev/msg08763.html
